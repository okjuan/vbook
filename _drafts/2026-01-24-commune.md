It was on Wednesday maybe Thursday when I started noticing how good a week I was having.
<!--more-->
It's been full of activities that have produced in me a feeling of communion.
Communion with friends, teammates, strangers, neighbors, family friends, acquantainces.
(Is it fair to say that even the hours I've spent reading have felt like hours in communion?)

Going to a concert is fun, playing games is fun, drawing and listening to music is fun, and it's fun to do all these things with other people, but there's something about knowing or even expecting that you'll see those people again that reinforces the feeling of connection.
The moment is by itself meaningful but it is something greater when you know it is a single episode in an ongoing series with a future unforeseeable and possible
