### feeling averse to travel

For at least a year now I've been craving stability and routine.
I suppose it's no surprise considering I turn thirty this summer.
I want to get into a rhythm and maintain it for stretches of time.
Work, play soccer, read, write, watch movies, spend time with friends, go out the odd evening, dedicate downtime to hobbies old and new.
