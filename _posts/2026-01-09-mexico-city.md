After {% vbook_post vacationing in the Yucatan Peninsula | 2025-12-16-yucatan %}, my younger sister and I went to Mexico City to visit family.
<!--more-->

#### Sunday 01/04/2026 -- Merida &#8594; Mexico City

- land & disembark @ 10:30am; take taxi to our aunt & uncle's house in el Pedregal
- drink coffee & chat w/ my uncle G
- watch second half of Chelsea vs Man City until my grandpa & my aunt T arrived
- sit & chat w/ my grandpa; drink a beer & a tequila w/ him while my cousins E & E & their dad J arrived
- eat an assortment of meats that my uncle G grilled: chistorra, Argentine chorizo, arrachera, & rib-eye
- drink coffee, eat cookies, & chat

#### Monday 01/05/2026 -- Mexico City (Palmas)

- sit in patio and write {% vbook_post _what I am doing with my life_ | 2026-01-05-my-life %} & other bits
- eat lunch @ kitchen table w/ my sister, my aunt C, & my cousin G
- hang out & chat in living room, drinking coffee & eating cookies
- watch _The Holdovers_ w/ my sister & cousin G
- supper @ Taqueria Los Atarantados

#### Tuesday 01/06/2026 -- Mexico City (El Pedregal & Santa Fe)

- play tennis w/ my sister & cousin G
- lunch @ Los Canarios in Perisur w/ my sister, my aunt C, & my cousin C
- dillydally @ Centro Santa Fe & then Parque La Mexicana
- supper & rosca de reyes @ my aunt T's house with aunts T, C, & S as well as several cousins & their partners

#### Wednesday 01/07/2026 -- Mexico City (Roma Norte)

- relax & drink coffee
- spend the afternoon w/ my grandpa at his senior's home w/ my sister & aunt C
- look at photo albums; eat lunch; play a board game; accompany my grandpa up to the gym
- meet w/ my childhood friend J
- beer @ La Roma Brewing then tacohopping @ Taquerias La Taque then @ Taqueria Los Atarantados

#### Thursday 01/08/2026 -- Mexico City (La Condesa) & Toluca

- go to Mexican Federation of Football w/ my uncle G, my cousin G, and my sister
- tour the office; get a tutorial from folks who run the VAR; watch bits of a youth match at one of the fields
- have pizza for lunch while watching Arsenal vs Liverpool on patio
- spend time w/ my grandpa; play a board game with him, my sister, & my aunt C
- check in for flight; struggle through app errors
- supper @ El Tizoncito in La Condesa w/ a group of cousins

#### Friday 01/09/2026 -- Mexico City

- wake @ 9am, say goodbye to my uncle, drink coffee, and journal
- breakfast @ kitchen table w/ my aunt C & cousin G
- more coffee & journaling; pack, shower, and leave for airport
