### finalizing our home purchase

Emotion overcame me when we signed the final document to complete the purchase of our condo.
It was beyond reasonable doubt now, my wife and I had bought our first home.
Every apartment I've rented I've loved furnishing and decorating and each one I've loved as a place of my own, but having a place truly of our own feels a different thing and a dream come true.

### doubting my football fandom

Z has pointed out before that my frugality and anticonsumerist attitude fail when it comes to buying Arsenal jerseys.
And she's right.
My love of football and the thrill of identifying with an external force makes me susceptible to the capitalist processes of wealth extraction.
In a way, being a fan is like being a gambler.
