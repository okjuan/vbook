#### Sunday 07/19/2026 -- Montreal &#8594; Vancouver

Foolishly, I booked our return flight on the day of the World Cup final.
The game is supposed to end one hour and twenty minutes before our departure time.
I'm consider going to the airport early to watch the whole game there.
Otherwise I will have to watch it elsewhere and hurry to the airport afterwards.
If the game is tied after ninety minutes there'll be another half hour of play and if it is still tied, penalties.
In that case, they'll be happening while we board the plane.
I hope I don't miss anything special and I regret not being able to watch the game with my friends at home.
